Asteroid RL

Asteroids are a kind of solar system minor bodies. They have various shapes, since they are not massive enougth to be spherical, so it is important to know their shapes to understand the solar system formation and evolution.
Lightcurve inversion is a method to reconstruct the shape of asteroids, using the change of flux as the asteroid rotates. It is also important in mathematics field, as it is a kind of inverse problem. This problem was first studied by Kaasalainen et al.(2001), and many researchers have developed various algorithms to solve this problem.

I am trying to solve this problem using reinforcement learning, which is a type of deep learning.
